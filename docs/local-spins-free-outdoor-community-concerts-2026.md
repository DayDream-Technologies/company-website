# FREE OUTDOOR COMMUNITY CONCERTS 2026

**EDITOR’S NOTE:** _Welcome to our 2026 listing of free outdoor summer concert series, shown alphabetically by city or town. The concerts are organized by municipalities or non-profit groups in public spaces and free to attend. More will be added to the list regularly as they’re confirmed. (Photo at Ada’s Legacy Park by Eric Stoike.)_

_Want to add a series to the list or update information? Email details to juliel100@protonmail.com. If you want to advertise your series in this guide or elsewhere at Local Spins, contact john@localspins.com_

_Get updated info about Michigan music festivals in our Michigan Music Festivals 2025 Guide._
